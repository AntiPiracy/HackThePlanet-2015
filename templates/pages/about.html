On The Anti-Piracy Video Tool

This is the uppcoming application that utilizes the art of steganography to fight piracy. The idea is to embed user information into a video; certain pixels within videos will get modified for such user data as usernames. This should be a subtle/not noticeable change (ideally the latter).
